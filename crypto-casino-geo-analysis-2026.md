## FINAL NOTES

1. **The single biggest insight:** Markets where domestic regulation is restrictive but enforcement against offshore/crypto operators is weak represent the best opportunities. Germany (deposit caps push players offshore), Brazil (crypto banned but everyone uses it), Nigeria (crypto solves a real payment problem), and UAE (new market with near-zero competition) are the four standout markets.

2. **Prediction markets** (Polymarket white-label) are a differentiator in markets where they're not explicitly banned. Brazil, Nigeria, Philippines, Vietnam, and Thailand have no specific prediction market regulation. Argentina and Singapore have explicitly banned them.

3. **PVP skill-based games** are a regulatory advantage in markets like India (skill games were historically exempt from gambling laws, though PROGA complicates this) and Germany (skill games may not fall under GlüStV restrictions).

4. **The Stake.com moat is real** — 52% market share. You don't compete with Stake on scale. You compete on: (a) markets they don't serve well (Nigeria, Vietnam, Thailand), (b) product verticals they're weaker in (prediction markets, PVP skill games), (c) better localization, and (d) community-first approach.

5. **Stablecoins (USDT) are the product-market fit**, not BTC. In every emerging market, users want dollar-stability with crypto speed. USDT on Tron (cheapest gas) should be the default payment method.
