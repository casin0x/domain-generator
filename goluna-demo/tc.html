T&C Clause Library — GoLuna

Bullet-pointed library of T&C clauses sourced from the Betplay.io and Betpanda.io whitelabels (same chassis as GoLuna). Each clause is annotated with its bankroll-protection role and source attribution. This is NOT a finalised T&C document — it is a working library for a lawyer to assemble into GoLuna’s official T&Cs. Adopt the clauses that fit, modify amounts, drop what doesn’t apply.

How to read this page.  Each section corresponds to a category of T&C clause. Clauses marked bankroll-critical are the ones that directly reduce the bankroll multiplier required (see Revenue §03.5). Standard operational clauses are marked standard. Adopting the bankroll-critical clauses cuts the M6 bankroll requirement from $680K (at 4×) to ~$510K (at 3×).
01 Withdrawals & Velocity Caps — Bankroll-Critical 8 clauses Bankroll-critical

The single biggest bankroll-multiplier reducer. By capping how fast a player can extract winnings, the bankroll only needs to cover daily withdrawal flow rather than the full winnings balance.

Daily withdrawal cap — $5,000 USD per 24 hours To maximise security in the event of an account being breached, users can withdraw cryptocurrency up to the value of $5,000 USD every 24 hours. A user's VIP level may increase the maximum withdrawal per day. Source: Betplay §4 ($8,000) — GoLuna adjusted to $5,000 given smaller bankroll
Weekly withdrawal cap — €15,000 per 7 days Cumulative withdrawals capped at €15,000 per rolling 7-day window. Limit can be adjusted by contacting support; VIP tiers may unlock higher limits. Source: Betpanda §4
Monthly withdrawal cap — €45,000 per calendar month Cumulative withdrawals capped at €45,000 per calendar month. Combined with daily and weekly caps, creates a layered velocity throttle. Source: Betpanda §4
Crypto-shortage delay clause — explicit operator safety valve We reserve the right to delay withdrawals in case of cryptocurrency shortages. This grants legal authority to defer payouts when bankroll is short. This is the single most powerful bankroll-protection clause in the playbook. Source: Betpanda §4 — direct lift recommended
1× deposit wagering before withdrawal All deposits are subject to a minimum wagering requirement of at least one time before any withdrawal requests can be processed. Players must place bets equivalent to the deposited amount at least once before initiating a withdrawal. Source: Betplay §3 + Betpanda §3
Withdrawals via the same method as deposit Withdrawals from the gaming account are made using the same method as was used for the deposit. We reserve the right to transfer funds in a way different from the payment method specified by the player. Source: Betpanda §4
Security check delays at our discretion Any withdrawal may be subjected to additional security checks at our discretion. This may cause delays in the transaction being processed and GoLuna cannot be held liable for any financial losses caused by this. Source: Betpanda §4
No recovery for misdirected crypto withdrawals In the unlikely event of a player withdrawing cryptocurrency funds to another cryptocurrency network address, GoLuna will not be responsible to recover the amount. Users are responsible for confirming and checking they are withdrawing to the right wallet address. Source: Betpanda §4
02 Maximum Win & Bet Caps — Worst-Case Exposure Limits 7 clauses Bankroll-critical

Hard caps on individual wins and bet sizes limit worst-case bankroll exposure on any single game outcome.

Max single-round win — €50,000 in equivalent crypto Winnings on any single round are capped at €50,000 in equivalent cryptocurrency. Any potential outcome that would exceed this limit cannot be achieved — the game engine enforces the cap regardless of theoretical pay multiplier. Source: Betplay §17.5 (€100,000) — GoLuna conservative at €50,000
Max 24-hour win — 3 BTC equivalent The maximum amount a player can win in a 24-hour period is 3 BTC or an equivalent amount in other cryptocurrencies. Source: Betpanda §5 (3,000,000 uBTC = 3 BTC)
Max single-betting-round win — 5 BTC equivalent The maximum amount a player can win in a single betting round is 5 BTC or an equivalent amount in other cryptocurrencies. Sportsbook bets that would pay more are capped at this maximum. Source: Betpanda §5 (10 BTC) — GoLuna at 5 BTC
Max bet on GoLuna Originals — €1,000 per spin/round The standard maximum bet limit across GoLuna Originals games is €1,000 in equivalent cryptocurrency. Bet-size cap directly limits worst-case single-bet exposure. Source: Betplay §17.4 (€5,000) — GoLuna conservative at €1,000
Max weekly cashback — €15,000 The maximum amount a player can receive in weekly cashback is the cryptocurrency equivalent of €15,000. Cashback is not applicable to sports betting, esports betting, or virtual sports betting. Source: Betpanda §5 (€20,000) — GoLuna at €15,000
Payouts rounded down to nearest whole cent All payouts are rounded down to the nearest whole cent in the selected currency. Small but adds up over volume. Source: Betplay §17.6
All bets final once initiated All bets are final once initiated and cannot be cancelled or refunded. You maintain sole responsibility for every wager placed through your account. Source: Betplay §17.11
03 Wagering Requirements 6 clauses Operational
Deposit wagering — 1× before any withdrawal All deposits must be wagered one time (1×) before you can withdraw any deposited funds. Withdrawals of funds that have not been wagered will not be accepted. Source: Betpanda §3 + Betplay §3
Welcome bonus wagering — 30× Welcome bonus money must be wagered 30 times before any associated winnings can be withdrawn. Wagering contributions: slots 100%, table games 10%, live casino 10%, sportsbook 0%. GoLuna recommended (industry standard 25–40×)
Reload bonus wagering — 25× Reload bonus money must be wagered 25 times before associated winnings can be withdrawn. Slightly lower than welcome bonus to reward repeat depositors. GoLuna recommended
Free spin winnings — 25× wagering Winnings from free spins are credited as bonus money and must be wagered 25 times before withdrawal. GoLuna recommended
Free-contest / giveaway winnings — 1× rollover + valid deposit history All winnings from free GoLuna contests are subject to a 1× rollover requirement before funds can be withdrawn. All winners must have a valid GoLuna account and must have made at least one ($10 minimum) deposit. Contest winnings expire after 30 days. Source: Betplay §14 (Promo/Free Contests)
Bonus expiry — 30 days from credit All bonus credits expire 30 days from issue if wagering requirements are not met. Forfeited bonus money plus any associated winnings revert to the house. GoLuna recommended
04 Anti-Fraud, Anti-Abuse & Forfeiture 9 clauses Bankroll-critical

Bankroll-relevant: industry practice 5–15% of would-be winnings get clawed back via these clauses. Material bankroll relief.

One account per person/household/IP/device Only one account per person, household, IP address and computer device is allowed. Creating multiple user accounts may result in termination of the accounts and suspending of all payouts. Source: Betpanda §5
Multi-account winnings reclaimable Any returns, winnings, or bonuses gained or accrued during such time as the duplicate account was active may be reclaimed by us. Should we decide to leave one account open, it will be the first account opened. Source: Betpanda §5
No bots, scripts, or automation You agree not to make use of any third-party programs, tools, or bots that automate and/or hack gameplay in any way. In the event of suspected use of third-party actions, GoLuna reserves the right to hold player withdrawal for further investigation, and may confiscate any winnings derived from usage. Source: Betpanda §1 + Betplay §1
Zero tolerance for advantage play GoLuna has zero tolerance for advantage play. Any player who tries to gain advantage of casino offers or promotions agrees that the company has the right to void bonuses and any winnings from such bonuses. Includes: creating multiple accounts, providing incorrect registration data, or any actions which may damage the casino. Source: Betpanda §5
No betting-technique circumvention It is not permitted to utilise or deploy any novel or recognised betting techniques while using our services which are designed to circumvent the standard house edge in our casino games nor the sportsbook. We may immediately suspend, terminate, or restrict access to your account and retain any funds. Source: Betpanda §5
Suspicion of fraud — payout suspension If a player is suspected of fraudulent actions (collusion, unfaithful winnings strategies, fraud against other casinos or payment providers, cheating, or known bankruptcy), the company reserves the right to terminate the account and suspend all payouts. Decision at sole discretion of the company. Source: Betpanda §5
Violation of terms — full balance forfeit In case of violation of these terms, GoLuna reserves the right to block and/or immediately close the account, and you lose all winnings/balance. Source: Betplay §1 + Betpanda §1
System manipulation — retention rights + criminal referral The casino reserves the right to retain payments if suspicion or evidence exists of manipulation of the casino system. Criminal charges will be brought against any user who has manipulated the casino system or attempted to do so. Source: Betpanda §5
Bypass attempts void all wagering In the event that you make use of any means intended to bypass any fraud, security controls, or any restrictions, all wagering activity (including bonuses, if any) will be considered null and void. Source: Betpanda §5

We reserve the right to lock your account or prevent withdrawals, if deemed appropriate, until such time as your identity has been verified. Source: Betpanda §6
Withhold payments pending verification We reserve the right to withhold any payment to/from your account until we are satisfied that the identity, age, and place of residence of the player has been suitably verified. Source: Betpanda §6
Player warranties — age, ownership, jurisdiction, legality of funds You warrant that: you are at least 18 years of age (or higher age required by applicable laws); you are the rightful owner of the money in your account; you reside in a jurisdiction which allows gambling; and the money deposited is not derived from any illegal activity. Source: Betpanda §6
AML compliance — suspicious transaction reporting The company complies with laws, regulations, and guidelines for the prevention of money laundering and the funding of terrorism. Suspicious transactions shall be investigated and reported to the competent FIU. In any such event, the company is prohibited from informing players or third parties involved. Source: Betpanda §6
Identity mismatch — forfeit winnings, return deposit If after verifying your identity it results that your account details do not match, we reserve the right to immediately close or suspend your account. Any winnings and bonuses will be confiscated; depending on circumstances, any remaining balance of the deposit returned. Source: Betpanda §6
