06 Launch Plan
1

Now -- Vendor Selection

Finalize vendor contracts and agree on package tiers. Resolve open questions from Section 07. Confirm account access and brand assets handover.

2

Week 1 -- Onboarding

Begin InClips Pilot ($25K) campaign setup. MAADS onboarding: account creation, tracking pixel integration, S2S postback configuration. Creative asset delivery.

3

Week 2 -- Live Campaigns

InClips logo placements go live across IG, X, TikTok. MAADS Web3 campaigns launch on Slise and AADS. First streamer sessions scheduled.

4

Week 3-4 -- Optimization

Review initial performance data. Adjust CPM bids and targeting. Scale performing channels. Cut underperformers. First KOL activations from MAADS roster.

5

Month 2 -- Scale Decision

Full performance review across both vendors. Decide on tier upgrades based on CPA and ROI metrics. Expand to Growth tier if unit economics hold.
