02Month-by-month — what gets paid, and when

Nothing except the setup fee is paid ahead of performance. Media is prepaid month by month as it runs; the 15% management fee only accrues on media actually spent; the retainer is monthly. Each month is its own tranche — “cut after month N” means exactly “stop paying from month N+1.”

04Conditions to write into the agreement

C1Hard cap — $100K all-in

Covers everything: setup, retainer, media, 15% management, WhatsApp messaging and payment fees, over the full 3-month pilot. Committed plan is $87.45K; anything above the cap needs a new written sign-off.

C2Month-1 exit clause

After the first live media month, GoLuna may terminate with no further obligation. Trigger: blended CAC above ~$30 (Anzil’s own quote is $12–25), fewer than ~500 FTDs on the $15K month, or the PIX→crypto on-ramp not converting deposits.

C3Retainer dies with the deal

The €1.5K/mo advisory retainer terminates the moment the engagement does — no tail, no notice period beyond the current month.

C4Scope: media only

Influencer activation and content clipping are out of scope. Media = Meta + WhatsApp, prepaid by GoLuna month-to-month. GoLuna owns the player and LTV data and decides where budget scales.

05Revenue targets — what the pilot must produce

Basis: ARPU $30/mo (Brazil regulated-market average), CAC $20 (Anzil’s quote), net contribution 51.5% of GGR after bonuses, providers, crypto, platform and a deliberately high PIX estimate. NEED = the break-even line (~28% retention — the M4-exit run-rate just covers the fixed burn). OPTIMIZE = ~39% retention, where the reinvest-all flywheel actually compounds.
