What Hotshot is NOT

  • Not a system where users compete against the platform
  • Not regulated under gambling laws in most jurisdictions

04 Channel Strategy
Three-phase approach: first create awareness (paid), then convert traffic (content + tournaments), then build long-term trust (PR + community).
Phase 1

Demand Generation

Create awareness and attract new users to the platform.

  • Paid ads on X, Meta, TikTok, Telegram
  • Google and Reddit banner placements
  • Crypto-focused ad networks (AADS + Slise)
  • On-chain targeting via Slise wallet inventory
  • KOL and influencer partnerships
  • Launch tournament for initial activation
Phase 2

Demand Capture

Convert traffic into active, retained users.

  • Short-form clips on TikTok (clipping campaign)
  • Gameplay highlights on YouTube
  • Streamer content for UGC pipeline
  • Launch tournament for initial engagement
  • Retargeting campaigns for non-converters
  • Email / wallet capture for remarketing
Phase 3

Trust & Authority

Build credibility and long-term brand equity.

  • PR articles on industry-relevant sites
  • Product reviews on Trustpilot, Google, Reddit
  • Organic Reddit community management
  • Partnership announcements
  • Investor / founder public backing
  • Ongoing social proof and testimonials

09 KOL & Influencer Strategy
Quality over quantity. One streamer with 200 viewers who brings 50 real players is worth more than a mega-influencer pushing empty impressions.

Micro-KOL Focus

  • Prioritize smaller streamers with authentic engagement
  • 200-300 viewers is enough if content quality is high
  • 50-100 strong players from a good streamer beats mass impressions
  • Quality of traffic is everything — not volume

Payment Model

  • Push for ambassador / rev-share deals first
  • Flat fee fallback if rev-share rejected
  • Hybrid: lower flat fee + higher percentage
  • Trial period: flat fee month 1, transition to rev-share

Content & Clipping

  • Streamers play Hotshot, creating raw gameplay content
  • Clips distributed across 50+ TikTok accounts
  • Target: 50-100 million impressions from clips
  • AADS handles clipping in-house (confirmed by Eva)

Regional Approach

  • Two landing pages per region (iGaming cross-sell vs pure Hotshot)
  • Strict regions: Hotshot-only branding (no iGaming references)
  • Relaxed regions: iGaming platform with Hotshot as entry point
  • Vietnam-specific: find local streamer with good persona
