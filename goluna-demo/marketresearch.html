Strategic Insights

"USDT on Tron is the product"

In every emerging market, users want dollar-stability with crypto speed. USDT on Tron is the cheapest transfer option and should be the default payment method. Stablecoins are the product-market fit, not BTC. Essential for Nigeria (Naira hedge), Argentina (dollar proxy), Turkey (Lira hedge), Brazil, and all of Southeast Asia.

"Don't compete with Stake on scale"

Stake.com has 52% market share and ~$10B/month in bets. You compete on: (a) markets they don't serve well (Nigeria, Vietnam, Thailand), (b) product verticals they're weaker in (prediction markets, PVP skill games), (c) better localization, and (d) community-first approach. The top 5 control ~80% of traffic.

"Germany's restrictions push players to you"

The GlüStV imposes a EUR 1,000/month deposit cap, 5-second spin timers, and max EUR 1 stake on slots. High-value German players are actively searching for "Casino ohne Limit" (casino without limits). These restrictions are a gift to offshore crypto casinos — Germany is already Europe's most active crypto gambling population.

"Prediction markets are the differentiator"

Polymarket + Kalshi control 97.5% of prediction market volume, but white-label prediction markets remain a wide-open niche. Markets with no explicit ban: Brazil, Nigeria, Philippines, Vietnam, Thailand, Kenya. Banned in: Argentina, Singapore, Australia. This is a product vertical where Stake is not dominant.

PVP Skill Games — Fast Transaction Gaming (FTG)

Every in-game action is a micro-transaction between players
Based on the RFUni/Hotshot model. Players enter arenas with real stakes. Each action — a hit, a kill, a capture — transfers value from one player to another in real time.
Shoot-to-Earn — players enter $1 / $2 / $5 arenas, each hit transfers value from victim to shooter
  • Revenue model: House takes rake on every transaction (not edge on outcomes)
  • Legal advantage: Skill-based = different regulatory classification than gambling in many jurisdictions
  • Distribution: Browser-based — no app store approval needed
