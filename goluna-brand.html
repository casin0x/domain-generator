Recommendation

Which direction serves GoLuna's multi-vertical ambition best?

Top Pick

Direction 1: Moonlit Luxe

Luxury positioning differentiates GoLuna from every neon-lit crypto casino. The moon metaphor gives you infinite content depth — moon phases for promotions, lunar cycles for VIP events, eclipse specials. Premium enough for sports, elegant enough for predictions, aspirational enough for high-rollers.

Runner-Up

Direction 3: Silver Tide

The calm confidence is a blue ocean (literally). While every casino screams, GoLuna whispers. Trust-forward brand that works perfectly for sports and predictions where credibility matters.

Wildcard

Direction 2: Neon Eclipse

If targeting Gen-Z and crypto-native audience, the cyberpunk night energy is unmatched. Risk: may feel too "casino" for sports/predictions crossover.
